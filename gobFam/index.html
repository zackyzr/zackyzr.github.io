About Us

GobFam is a one-of-a-kind deflationary NFT art collection featuring unique Goblin characters called GOBs on the Cardano Blockchain. Every individual GOB NFT is backed by an asset (ADA) in the treasury. Each GOB is hand-drawn by our artist and comes with its own distinctive set of traits that are different from one another.

“Made by NFT collectors for NFT collectors”

-GobFam

As GobFam, we seek to create and promote a healthy NFT community where the needs and wants of GobFams are prioritized. We believe that no project can truly succeed without the support of its community. Thus, the GobTeam will run this project by putting our community first.

As NFT collectors our very own, the GobTeam understands that the participation in NFT projects involves uncertainties and possible risks. With the goal of minimizing these uncertainties, the GobTeam has designed multiple plans and mechanisms that would benefit the community at large.
